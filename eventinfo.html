What's included?

Step into a slow, sunlit afternoon at Greenhouse Corner, where tea is poured gently and conversation comes easy. Surrounded by soft greenery and warm light, guests are invited to settle in, sip, and stay awhile. The space is designed to feel calm, welcoming, and quietly beautiful—an easy pause from the rush of the day. Each pour is meant to be savored slowly, encouraging you to settle in and enjoy the moment.

What's included?

Alongside your tea, a selection of seasonal pastries and light bites will be served, offering a balance of sweet and savory. Freshly prepared and simply styled, each item adds to the ease and warmth of the afternoon. Guests will also have access to the full garden space and seating throughout the event. Whether you choose to gather at a table or wander with your cup in hand, the space is yours to enjoy.

What should I wear?.

We invite guests to dress in garden casual or semi-formal attire, keeping comfort and ease in mind. Think light, breathable fabrics, soft layers, and silhouettes that feel relaxed yet put together.
The setting is meant to feel natural and unhurried, and your attire can reflect that same sense of ease. Whether simple or slightly dressed up, the goal is to feel at home in the space.
Florals, soft tones, and gentle textures are especially encouraged, echoing the greenery and warmth of Greenhouse Corner. Feel free to lean into the charm of the afternoon with subtle accessories or a touch of personal style.
While dressing up is never required, it can add to the experience and make  the day feel just a bit more special. Above all, come as you are and enjoy the moment.
